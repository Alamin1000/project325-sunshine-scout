How it Works

When you sign up for Sunshine Scout, our service will automatically check the weather forecast for your location every day. If we find a day with at least 6 consecutive hours of great weather, we'll send you an SMS alert so you don't miss out on the perfect sunny day.

You can customize your alert preferences by expanding the hidden section in the form above. Set your desired minimum consecutive hours of sunshine, preferred alert time, minimum temperature, and maximum rain chance to get personalized alerts that fit your needs.
